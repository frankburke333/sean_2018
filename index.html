What a summer! A great party on the waterfront at Cabana, an Ice Cream truck at the office, Montreal Birthday Bagels and to cap it all off Sharlene and Daniel throw a fantastic party to close out the season on what was the nicest day we've had all year! On behalf of all of us who attended, thanks so much! Here is a preview of upcoming events you can look forward to:


  • Thanksgiving Lunch - We will be bringing in Portuguese Chicken from Bairrada Churrasqueira for lunch to celebrate Thanksgiving together on Thur Oct 5th. We will send the invite shortly

  • Pumpkin Carving - We will be carving pumpkins and sharing a snack in the days leading up to Halloween. Invite will go out soon.

  • Halloween Costumes - On Oct 31st you are welcome to wear a costume. We will have those who dressed up get together and judges will award a prize to the best costume.

  • Holiday Party - We will be having our holiday party on Thursday Dec 14th at Church Aperitivo Bar. This is a great site with excellent food and dancing. More Details and invite to follow
